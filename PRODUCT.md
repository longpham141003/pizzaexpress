# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Customers in Vietnam who want to order delicious, fresh, and hot pizza quickly for delivery or take-away. They are looking for value (special promos/combos) and need an effortless ordering workflow.

## Product Purpose
Allow users to browse the menu, customize pizza sizes/variations, view locations and reviews, and order online smoothly.

## Brand Personality
Vibrant, Appetizing, Trustworthy, Friendly.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered grids with overlapping text.
- Low-contrast elements (e.g. light gray on white).
- Position-dependent layout elements that break when content changes dynamically.

## Design Principles
1. **Appetizing Presentation**: Put high-quality, descriptive food imagery front and center.
2. **Frictionless Customization**: Ensure selecting pizza sizes and adding to cart is intuitive and fast.
3. **Robust Alignment**: Maintain strict visual grid alignment (e.g., 4 columns) across all screen widths.
4. **Accessible Contrast**: Keep text colors highly legible against their backgrounds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG 2.1 AA standards for text contrast.
- Minimum touch targets of 44x44px for buttons, quantity inputs, and tab navigation.
